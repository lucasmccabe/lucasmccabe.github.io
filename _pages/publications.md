The primary thrust of my research pertains to **mathematical data science** and **natural language processing** (NLP), especially in pursuit of **trustworthy AI** systems. 
Recent work has examined **uncertainty quantification** (<a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2509.14478">ICLR '26</a>, <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00668">arXiv '26</a>) and obfuscated **evil twin prompts** (<a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.02273">EMNLP ‘25</a>, <a href="https://aclanthology.org/2024.emnlp-main.4">EMNLP '24</a>).
I am also interested in **interdisciplinary applications of data and network science**.
Such efforts have included a network analysis of opioid-involved mobility (<a href="https://doi.org/10.1007/s41109-024-00661-z">Applied Network Science '24</a>) and software for graph diffusion source inference (<a href="https://joss.theoj.org/papers/10.21105/joss.04894">JOSS '22</a>).

`*` indicates that the work has been accepted, but not yet published.
