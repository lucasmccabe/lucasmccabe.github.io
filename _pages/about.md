###### **Professional:**

I am a senior data scientist at <a href="https://www.lmi.org/">LMI</a>, working on applied research sponsored by <a href="https://www.darpa.mil">DARPA</a>, <a href="https://www.nrl.navy.mil/">NRL</a>, and others.

###### **Background:**
