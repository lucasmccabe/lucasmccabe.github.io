I am broadly interested in **mathematical data science** and **natural language processing** (NLP), especially in pursuit of **trustworthy AI** systems. 
Recent work has advanced variability-based **uncertainty quantification** (<a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/2509.14478">ICLR '26</a>) and examined **prompt obfuscation** (<a href="https://aclanthology.org/2024.emnlp-main.4">EMNLP '24</a>, <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.02273">EMNLP ‘25</a>) in LLMs.
I am also interested in interdisciplinary topics related to **computational social science**, often employing tools from **network science**. Projects herein have included a network analysis of opioid-involved overdose journeys (<a href="https://doi.org/10.1007/s41109-024-00661-z">Applied Network Science '24</a>) and development of graph diffusion source inference software (<a href="https://joss.theoj.org/papers/10.21105/joss.04894">JOSS '22</a>).

`*` indicates that the work has been accepted, but not yet published.
